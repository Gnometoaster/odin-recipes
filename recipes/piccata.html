Chicken Piccata

Description

This chicken piccata recipe is quick and easy. Pounded chicken breasts are topped with a simple pan sauce made with capers, butter, white wine, and lemon juice.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • salt, cayenne and black pepper
  • Flour
  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 1 tbs capers, drained
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 tbs unsalted butter
  • 2 tbs parsley

Steps

  1. Pound chicken breast to 1/2in thickness
  2. season with salt pepper and cayenne, dredge in flour
  3. Heat olive oil in skillet, cook chicken for 5 minutes on both sides; remove from heat
  4. Cook capers in reserved oil and smash lightly
  5. Reduce white wine in hot skillet for 2 minutes
  6. stir lemon juice, water and butter into white wine for 2 minutes
  7. return chicken breast to pan and cook until heated
